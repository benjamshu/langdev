Frontmatter

CLASSICAL SEVENSI is the form of SEVENSI in use from the beginning of written records through the publishing of the varous Classics. In addition to providing the basis for SEVENSI as a whole, many aspects of CLASSICAL SEVENSI are preserved in the "classical" speech-level.

This dictionary records the lexicon for CLASSICAL SEVENSI X, the first level of formalization and standardization from PROTO–JASTU-SEVENSI.
